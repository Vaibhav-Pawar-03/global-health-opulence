## Tips for Best Results

### Image Specifications
- **Hero images**: 1920x800px or larger (16:9 aspect ratio works well)
- **Medical destination cards**: 800x600px minimum (4:3 or 3:2 aspect ratio)
- **Experience cards**: 600x400px minimum (3:2 aspect ratio)
- **Format**: JPG preferred for photos (smaller file size)
- **Quality**: Download "Large" or "Original" from Unsplash

### Search Tips
1. Use the exact search queries provided for consistent results
2. Look for images with good composition and clear subjects
3. Prefer images with natural lighting
4. Avoid overly filtered or heavily edited images
5. Check that images are free from watermarks

### Optimization
After downloading, consider using tools like:
- [TinyJPG](https://tinyjpg.com) - Compress without losing quality
- [Squoosh](https://squoosh.app) - Advanced image optimization
